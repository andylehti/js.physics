Orbital Paths Guide & Simulation Goal

[- HIDE]

This simulation runs a full N-body calculation to model our solar system. You will see three orbital paths:

- BLUE lines show "perfect" Keplerian orbits, where only the Sun's gravity is considered.
- GREEN lines show the calculated path using real-world masses, including all gravitational interactions. This is the baseline for reality.
- RED lines trace the path using the masses you've set in the controls. This is your experiment.

Your goal is to adjust the masses to make the red lines follow the ideal blue lines more closely than the green lines do. The Stability Score measures how much better (positive) or worse (negative) your configuration is compared to the real-world baseline.
